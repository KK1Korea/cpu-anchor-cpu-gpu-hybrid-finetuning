  ■ FINDING — bf16 = fp32 CONFIRMED:
    - 500 steps, every step within ±0.002. Identical trajectory.
    - 7B's 22% improvement (Exp F: fp32→bf16) was NOT from fp32 superiority.
      The improvement came from "precision transition" — the ACT of switching.
    - sub-1.0 achieved without anchor → 7B's sub-1.0 barrier was QLoRA bottleneck.
    - This is the first known bf16 vs fp32 full-training comparison with
      identical seed on the same LLM (no quantization, pure bf16 vs pure fp32).
